As of July 2009, Rodovid had over 320,000 total records for individuals and families across all languages, including 65,000 in each of English and Russian.<ref>{{en icon}} [http://en.rodovid.org/wk/Rodovid_Statistics The Rodovid statistics page]</ref><ref>[http://en.rodovid.org/wk/Main_Page Rodovid.org English Main Page], retrieved July 31, 2009</ref> <ref>[http://ru.rodovid.org/wk/Многоязычное_генеалогическое_древо Russian Main Page], retrieved October 7, 2009]</ref> The Rodovid project's data and descriptions are stored on servers in Kiev, and available under the [[Creative Commons Attribution]] license where not already in the public domain. It has experimented with supporting imports of [[GEDCOM]] files.
