Its name comes from the [[De Bruijn sequence]], which can be considered a special case where ''n'' is 1 (one dimension). One of the main open questions regarding De Bruijn tori is whether a De Bruijn torus for a particular alphabet size can be constructed for a given ''m'' and ''n''.
