[[Packet (information technology)|Packet]]s consist of control information for addressing and routing, and a [[payload (software)|payload]] consisting of user data. The control information in IPv6 packets is subdivided into a mandatory fixed header and optional extension headers. The payload of an IPv6 packet is typically a [[datagram]] or segment of the higher-level [[Transport Layer]] protocol, but may be data for an [[Internet Layer]] (e.g., [[ICMPv6]]) or [[Link Layer]] (e.g., [[OSPF]]) instead.
