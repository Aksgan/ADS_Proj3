Then, starting from the central box of the first row with the number 1 (or the first number of any arithmetic progression), the fundamental movement for filling the boxes is diagonally '''up and right''' ('''↗'''), one step at a time. When a move would leave the square, it is wrapped around to the last row or first column, respectively.
