The name for the website was chosen by voting in April 2008 by readers of ''Coding Horror'', Atwood's popular programming blog.<ref name="help_name">{{cite web | title = Help Name Our Website | url = http://www.codinghorror.com/blog/archives/001101.html | work = Coding Horror | date = 2008-04-06 | accessdate = 2009-06-15 | author = Jeff Atwood}}</ref> On September 15, 2008 it was announced the public beta was in session and that the general public was now able to use it to seek assistance on programming related issues. {{As of|2011|01}}, Stack Overflow has about 450,000 registered users and 1.2 million questions. Based on [[tag (metadata)|tags]] assigned to the questions, the most common topics on the site are [[C Sharp (programming language)|C#]], [[Java (programming language)|Java]], [[PHP]], [[.NET Framework|.NET]], [[ASP.NET]], [[JavaScript]], [[C++]], [[jQuery]], [[iPhone SDK|iPhone]], and [[Python (programming language)|Python]].<ref name="tags">{{cite web | work = Stack Overflow | title = Tags | url= http://stackoverflow.com/tags | accessdate = 15 July 2010}}</ref> === Server Fault === In April 2009, the owners of ''Stack Overflow'' began a private beta for ''[[Server Fault]]'', a sister site.
