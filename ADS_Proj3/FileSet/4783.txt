Once on Wikia, wiki communities have complained of inappropriate advertisements, or advertising in the body text area.<ref>{{cite news|url=http://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/2008/sep/25/wikipedia.internet|title=Read me first: Wikipedia isn't about human potential, whatever Wales says|author=Finkelstein, Seth|publisher=''[[The Guardian]]''|date=2008-09-25 | location=London}}</ref> There is no easy way for individual communities to switch to conventional paid hosting, as Wikia usually owns the relevant domain names. In June 2008, Wikia adopted a new skin, ''Monaco'', intending to implement it as the default on almost all hosted wikis.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/Wikia%27s_New_Style|title=Wikia's New Style|author=Wikia, Inc.|date=2008-06-17|accessdate=2009-06-20}}</ref> The skin had an uneven reception, with issues over the prominent branding, in-content format-altering ads, and the mandatory nature of the change.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/Forum:Wikia%27s_New_Style/Archive_1|title=Forum:Wikia's New Style - Archive 1|accessdate=2009-06-20}}</ref> Many wiki users felt the choice of skin default should remain their own. The switch went ahead, but some wikis retained ''Monobook'' as their default. In September 2008, the Transformers Wikia moved their content to their own server, citing the format-altering ads and mandatory changes as reasons for their departure.<ref>[http://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/2008/jul/31/wikipedia "How will Wikia cope when the workers all quit the plantation?"] @ guardian.co.uk</ref><ref>[http://tfwiki.net/wiki/Press_Release:_Teletraan-1_Wikia_moves_to_TFWiki.net "Teletraan-1 Wikia moves to TFWiki.net" press release]</ref> In May 2009, Wikia removed the ability of individual users to choose a skin other than ''Monaco'' or ''Monobook'', claiming a testing burden and relative lack of features. Soon after, Wikia removed the option to set the default skin to ''Monobook'', with an exemption of certain large wikis, namely, Uncyclopedia.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/Forum:Changes_to_skin_preferences|title=Forum:Changes to skin preferences|date=2009-05-19|accessdate=2010-02-09}}</ref> In August 2010, Wikia announced a new mandatory skin change, this time to a new look nicknamed "Oasis".<ref name="WikiaBlog1">{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/User_blog:Sannse/Your_First_Look_at_the_New_Wikia|title=Your First Look at the New Wikia|work=Wikia|accessdate=5 September 2010}}</ref> The new skin omitted some features, such as the popular shoutbox.<ref name="WikiaNewSkinFAQ">{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/Wikia%E2%80%99s_new_look_-_FAQ#What_features_are_being_phased_out_completely_with_the_new_skin.3F|title=Wikia’s new look - FAQ|work=Wikia|accessdate=5 September 2010}}</ref> On September 23, 2010, Wikia introduced the new skin in public beta with the option to be the default skin for certain wikis, like Muppet Wiki.<ref name="wikiapublicbeta">{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/User_blog:Susan_Taylor/Experience_the_new_Wikia|title=Experience the new Wikia|work=Wikia|accessdate=23 September 2010}}</ref> Wikia also revealed the official name of the new skin, ''Wikia''.<ref>{{cite web|url=http://community.wikia.com/wiki/The_new_look_2|title=The new look 2|date=2010-09-23|accessdate=2010-09-23}}</ref> The ''Wikia'' skin became the default skin on Wikia on November 3, 2010.
