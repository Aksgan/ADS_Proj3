The task is to assign an [[electronic document]] to one or more [[Categorization|categories]], based on its contents. Document classification tasks can be divided into two sorts: '''supervised document classification''' where some external mechanism (such as human feedback) provides information on the correct classification for documents, and '''unsupervised document classification''', where the classification must be done entirely without reference to external information.
