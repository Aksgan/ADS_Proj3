The area between axis and line are commonly emphasized with colors, textures and hatchings. * [[Scatter plot]] - type of mathematical diagram using [[Cartesian coordinates]] to display values as collection of points for two variables for a set of data. * [[Radar chart|Circular charts]] - [[Radar chart]] (Spider chart) or [[Pie chart#Polar area pie chart|Polar chart]] is a two-dimensional chart of three or more quantitative variables represented on axes starting from the same point. * [[Population pyramid|Accumulation charts]] - Pyramid (Cone) or Funnel chart displays data that, when combined, equals 100%. * [[Treemapping|Tree/Heat map]] - chart where the areas of the rectangles correspond to values.
