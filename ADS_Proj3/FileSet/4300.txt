at centrally managed corporate environments security for [[Server (computing)|servers]] and [[workstations]]. ==Version history== Endpoint 11 is the successor to '''Symantec AntiVirus Corporate Edition'''. Endpoint 11 adds handling of spyware and support for endpoint security health verification, among other features. The last version of Symantec AntiVirus was version 10, and Endpoint Protection begins at version 11. In April 2009, Symantec Endpoint Protection Small Business Edition was released, catering towards businesses smaller than the enterprise, but featuring the same underlying protection technologies. ==System requirements== Endpoint 11 supports [[Windows 7]], [[Windows Server 2008]], [[Windows Server 2003]], [[Windows Vista]], [[Windows XP]], and [[Windows 2000]]. Windows 7 is officially supported in the 11.0.5 release. There is an antivirus [[client (computing)|client]] for various distributions of [[Linux]] as well.
